Stress Reduction During the Holidays

As if life isn’t stressful enough on a daily/weekly/monthly basis anyway, here come the holidays again! Yes, there is the shopping, depleting cash resources and/or increasing credit card debt. And there is trying to please everyone on your list. The thing we love and dread the most is the infamous, mandatory family gatherings. You have to spend hours with your father, who is very sarcastic, or with your mother who loves to criticize. Perhaps your sister who always mentions your weight will be at dinner, counting your calories; or your brother who holds a different and very opinionated political opinion will show up. You can walk into these situations with calmness and peace in your heart. Bring down your inner heat with L.I.F.E. System Biofeedback Device stress-reduction sessions. A common experience during a session is tremendous relaxation. Afterward a common comment is “I feel lighter.” If you want to feel “lighter” going into your holiday festivities, contact Barbara for your biofeedback sessions.
